About Us


Courserating.elasticbeanstalk.com is the online destination for course ratings. This site can be used for quickly researching and rating courses in different colleges and universities across the United States. Currently this site is used for course rating of only San Jose State University.Here students can comment on and rate their courses.


This website is built for college students, by college students. Choosing the best courses is a rite of passage for every student, and connecting with peers on the site has become a key way for millions of students to navigate this process. The site does what students have been doing forever - checking in with each other – their friends, their brothers, their sisters, their classmates – to figure out which is a better course according to their interest.
